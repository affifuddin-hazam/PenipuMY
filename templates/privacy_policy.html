Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Welcome to PenipuMY. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Display name, profile picture, user handle
  • Authentication Data: Google OAuth tokens, Telegram user IDs (if using social login)
  • Contact Information: Email address for communications

Scam Report Data

When you submit a scam report, we collect:

  • Phone numbers, bank account numbers, and social media URLs of reported scammers
  • Description of the scam incident
  • Loss amount (if applicable)
  • Evidence screenshots and documents
  • Timestamp and IP address of submission

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Search queries, pages viewed, features used
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication and user preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our scam database and verification services
  • Process and verify scam reports submitted by users
  • Enable community collaboration and fraud prevention
  • Communicate with you about your account and platform updates
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and illegal activities
  • Comply with legal obligations and law enforcement requests

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Public Information

Certain information is publicly visible to protect the community:

  • Scammer profiles (phone numbers, bank accounts, verified reports)
  • Non-anonymous comments and community input
  • Public statistics and aggregated data

Third-Party Services

We integrate with external verification services:

  • SemakMule (PDRM): For police report verification
  • Truecaller: For phone number verification
  • ToyyibPay: For processing donations

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, legal process, or legitimate requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted password storage using bcrypt hashing
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Cloudflare protection against DDoS and malicious traffic

Note: While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Anonymous Posting: Choose to submit reports or comments anonymously

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Scam reports and profiles are retained indefinitely to maintain the integrity of our fraud database and protect the community.

7. Children's Privacy

PenipuMY is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
